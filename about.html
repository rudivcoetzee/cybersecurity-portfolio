My Journey in Cybersecurity

Hello! I'm a passionate cybersecurity enthusiast and student with particular interest in ethical hacking, penetration testing, developing systems, and event management. My journey began when I discovered my fascination with understanding how systems work and how they can be broken.

I've studied for 3 years in the IT field, and then specialized in my deep interest, cybersecurity. My dream is to work across various sectors including finance, healthcare, and government, helping organizations strengthen their security posture and protect sensitive data from threats.

I believe that security is not just about implementing tools and technologies, but about fostering a culture of awareness and responsibility. My approach combines technical expertise with strategic thinking to develop comprehensive security solutions that address both current and emerging threats.

When I'm not hunting for vulnerabilities, I'm creating webpages and coding all around as I also enjoy development.
